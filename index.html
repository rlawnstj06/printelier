Questions, answered

How much does a custom piece cost?

Most pieces land between $15 and $60. Larger or intricate work (big lithophane lamps, multi-part decor) can be more. You always get an exact price with your free preview, before any payment.

How do I pay?

Interac e-Transfer once you approve your preview, or cash at pickup. If you found us on Facebook Marketplace, you can complete the purchase there instead, whatever feels most comfortable.

How long will my order take?

Most pieces are designed, printed and finished within 3 to 7 days. If you have a hard date (a birthday, a shower), tell us in the form and we will confirm it is doable before you commit.

Do you ship, or pickup only?

Free pickup in Vancouver. Local delivery is available for a small fee, and we can ship anywhere in Canada via Canada Post at cost.

What are the pieces made of? Safe for kids?

We print primarily in PLA, a rigid plant-based plastic that is sturdy and lightweight. Decorative pieces are fine anywhere; for toys, we design without small detachable parts and recommend supervised play for children under 3, as with any small object.

Can I just buy from your Facebook listings?

Absolutely. Ready-made pieces are on Facebook Marketplace. This site is for the custom work: when you want a name, a photo or an idea made real, start here.
