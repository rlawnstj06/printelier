Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

Printelier is a one-person custom 3D printing studio in Vancouver, BC. This page explains, in plain language, what happens to your information when you contact us. Short version: we only use it to make and deliver your order, and we never sell it.

What we collect

When you submit the order form, we receive the name, email, and order details you type in. If you email us, we receive whatever you send. That is all. We do not ask for payment details on this website.

Why we collect it

Only to reply to you, prepare your free design preview, quote a price, and complete your order. Nothing else.

How it is handled

  • Form messages are delivered to our email through Formspree, a form-handling service.
  • If website analytics are enabled, aggregate, anonymous visit data (pages viewed, rough region) may be collected to understand what is useful. This is not tied to your name.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone, ever.

Your choices

You can ask us to delete your information at any time. Just email us and it is gone.
